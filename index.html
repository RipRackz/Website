The Developer

About Rackz Optimizer

Built by a gamer who got tired of the industry's direction — for everyone who feels the same way.

Rackz
18 y/o solo developer  ·  Sweden

Growing up with budget hardware teaches you one thing fast — if you want performance, you have to squeeze it out yourself. Around 2023 I got into optimization utilities and it clicked immediately: simple tools, cheap price, real results. The scene was good.

Then it wasn't. Tools got expensive. Features got locked. And the part that genuinely baffled me — your tweaks get removed when your subscription expires. You're not buying a result, you're renting it. Pay or lose everything you set up. That's not optimization, that's a hostage situation.

So I built my own. What started as something for me and my friends turned into something I wanted to share with everyone. The rules were set from day one: user-friendly, affordable, and honest. When you apply a tweak, it stays applied — whether you keep the subscription or not. Whether you uninstall or not. What you applied is yours to keep.

Everything in this program was designed, built, and tested by one person. That means decisions are made by someone who actually uses it — not a team chasing a roadmap. Updates will be focused and intentional, and anything that hurts performance gets fixed fast.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you download.

When will Rackz Optimizer release?
Rackz Optimizer is scheduled to release in mid to late April. Follow the Discord server for real-time updates and early access announcements.
Do my tweaks get removed if I uninstall the program?
No. Every tweak you apply modifies Windows settings directly — they stay active permanently until you manually revert them. Uninstalling Rackz Optimizer has no effect on settings you've already applied. Your optimizations are yours to keep.
Is it safe to use?
Yes. Rackz Optimizer has been tested on multiple machines with both Intel & AMD CPUs and NVIDIA & Radeon GPUs with only positive results. Tweaks that carry any risk are clearly marked with a warning indicator so you can make an informed decision before enabling them.
How much will it cost?
Rackz Optimizer costs $6.99/month or $69.99 for a lifetime license — one-time payment, own it forever. Both plans include full access to all 260+ tweaks with no feature tiers. Tweaks you apply stay active permanently regardless of which plan you choose.
What hardware is supported?
Rackz Optimizer supports Windows 10 and Windows 11 on both Intel and AMD CPUs, with NVIDIA and AMD Radeon GPUs. The tweak library covers hardware-specific optimizations for all major configurations.
How often will the program be updated?
Rackz Optimizer is developed and maintained by a single person, so updates will be focused and intentional rather than constant. Any update that addresses a performance regression or serious bug will be prioritized and pushed quickly.
Can I revert tweaks after applying them?
Yes. Every toggle in the app can be switched back off at any time. Disabling a tweak restores the original Windows setting — no reinstall or manual registry editing required.
